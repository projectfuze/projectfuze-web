# Interview Guide — Founder / CEO

**What**: 45-minute structured interview with the company founder or CEO.
**When**: Week 1, Mon–Tue of the Scan.
**Who**: David conducts. Recorded (with permission) for AI processing.
**Output**: Structured transcript → themes, pain points, contradictions, quotable moments.

---

## Setup

- Duration: 45 minutes
- Recording: Yes (remind at start)
- Tone: Conversational but structured. Let them talk. Listen for contradictions.
- Goal: Understand what "working" looks like to leadership, where frustration lives, and what they think the problem is (vs. what it actually is).

---

## Interview Flow

### Opening (3 min)

"Thanks for making time. This is a diagnostic conversation — I'm trying to understand how your team actually works, not how it's supposed to work. There are no wrong answers. I'll ask direct questions. Feel free to push back."

### Section 1: Vision & Goals (10 min)

1. **"What's the company trying to accomplish in the next 12 months?"**
   - Listen for: clarity vs. vagueness, single focus vs. scattered priorities

2. **"How does that translate to what teams are working on right now?"**
   - Listen for: clear chain from goals → work, or a gap

3. **"If I asked three different team leads what the top priority is, would they give me the same answer?"**
   - Listen for: confidence or hesitation. This reveals alignment health.

### Section 2: How Decisions Get Made (10 min)

4. **"Walk me through a recent product decision — from idea to 'we're doing this.'"**
   - Listen for: who was involved, how long it took, where it was documented

5. **"Where do decisions actually live? If someone joins next month, how do they find out why you built X?"**
   - Listen for: Slack, meetings, someone's head, a doc no one reads

6. **"What's a decision you made recently that you had to re-explain multiple times?"**
   - Listen for: signal loss, context not traveling

### Section 3: Team & Execution (10 min)

7. **"What's the biggest gap between 'we decided to do X' and 'X shipped'?"**
   - Listen for: spec quality, handoff breaks, review bottlenecks, scope creep

8. **"Where does work get stuck most often?"**
   - Listen for: specific bottleneck or vague "everything takes too long"

9. **"How do you know if a sprint/cycle went well?"**
   - Listen for: metrics, vibes, founder checking in constantly

### Section 4: AI & Tools (7 min)

10. **"What's your current thinking on AI in your org?"**
    - Listen for: excitement vs. skepticism, strategy vs. experimentation

11. **"Who's using AI most effectively on your team? What are they doing?"**
    - Listen for: individual heroes vs. systemic adoption

12. **"What would change if your team could ship 3x faster without hiring?"**
    - Listen for: what they'd do with the leverage — reveals real priorities

### Closing (5 min)

13. **"What's the one thing you wish your team did differently?"**
    - Listen for: echoes of the intake packet answer, new themes

14. **"Anything I should know that I haven't asked about?"**

---

## Post-Interview Processing

Feed recording to AI agent with prompt:
1. Structured transcript with timestamps
2. Key themes (max 5)
3. Pain points (explicit and implicit)
4. Contradictions (what they said vs. what they revealed)
5. Quotable moments (for the report)
6. Preliminary staging hypothesis: what stage is this org at?
