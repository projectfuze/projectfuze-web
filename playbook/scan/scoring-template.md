## Scoring Guidance

Refer to [Assessment Dimensions](../framework/assessment-dimensions.md) for detailed rubric per dimension per score level. When in doubt:

- **0–1**: No system, or system exists on paper only
- **2**: System exists but requires constant human intervention
- **3**: System works and agents can participate meaningfully
- **4**: System runs with minimal human oversight
- **5**: System self-improves

Score what IS, not what COULD BE. Be honest. Clients respect a tough score more than a generous one.
