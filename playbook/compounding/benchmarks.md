# Industry Benchmarks

**What**: Accumulated scoring data from engagements to establish industry baselines.
**When**: Updated after every Scan. Becomes meaningful after 5+ engagements.
**Who**: David updates from retrospective data.
**Output**: "Average Series A SaaS scores X.X" — credibility data for Scan presentations.

---

## How to Use

After 5+ Scans, reference these benchmarks during client presentations:
- "Organizations like yours typically score between X and Y"
- "The most common bottleneck we see is Z"
- "Your engineering is above average, but your product alignment is below"

**Do not reference benchmarks until 5+ data points exist.** Small samples mislead.
